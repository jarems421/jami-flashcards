Final Star System (Locked)

No lines.
No automatic patterns.
User-controlled placement only.

Names are final:

Star → Ascended Star → Transcendent Star

🌟 1) Star

Earned: Every completed goal
Purpose: Momentum & habit reinforcement

Meaning

“I showed up and did the work.”

Visual rules

Soft white glow

Symmetrical radial rays

Very subtle dust/particles

No animation (or an extremely slow pulse)

Behavior

Instantly added on goal completion

Appears where the user places it

No ceremony, no interruption

This is the background rhythm of the constellation.

⭐ 2) Ascended Star

Earned: Every 10 goals
Purpose: Consistency milestone

Meaning

“This is no longer accidental — I’m progressing.”

Visual differences (from Star)

Slight warm tint (ivory / pale gold / soft blue)

Clear secondary halo (outer glow)

Rays slightly longer and less uniform

Gentle breathing glow (slow, calm)

Behavior

Small focus animation on unlock

Copy:

“10 goals completed — you earned an Ascended Star.”

Then it’s placed exactly where the user wants

Ascended Stars should feel earned, not flashy.

✨ 3) Transcendent Star

Earned: Every 25 goals
Purpose: Rare achievement / identity marker

Meaning

“This effort has changed me.”

Visual identity (distinct, not louder)

Bright, crisp core with clear falloff

Non-uniform rays (varied angles/lengths)

Subtle lens-flare cross (thin, sharp)

Faint cool edge tones (silver / cyan / violet)

ONE calm motion only (slow rotation or slow particle emission)

Behavior

Brief full-screen focus on unlock

Copy:

“25 goals completed — a Transcendent Star joins your constellation.”

Then full manual placement by the user

These should feel rare, deliberate, and memorable.

🎛️ Manual Placement Rules (core to the design)

This is important — your instinct here is very good.

All stars are freely movable

No snapping

No enforced clusters

No auto-layout

The constellation reflects personal meaning, not algorithmic structure

This turns the constellation into:

a visual diary of effort

Not a leaderboard. Not a game map.

Tier	How you recognize it instantly
Star	Plain white glow
Ascended Star	Warm tint + halo
Transcendent Star	Unique structure + motion

Even without tapping, users should know.
